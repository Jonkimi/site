# Least Absolute Deviations and Least Squares [^1]

| Least Absolute Deviations | Least Squares |
| :---: | :---: |
| 最小一乘 | 最小二乘 |
| $$ S=\\sum_{x=0}^n &#124; y_i - f(x_i)&#124; $$ | $$ S=\\sum_{x=0}^n ( y_i - f(x_i))^2 $$ |
